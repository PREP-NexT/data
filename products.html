Deliverables/Products¶

The GDFC delivers relevant products, including continental drought and pluvial catalogues, global-scale drought and flood (pluvial and fluvial) risk maps, long-term meteorological and agricultural standardized indices, underlying meteorological forcings and land surface hydrological fluxes and states [Table 1].

Continental drought and pluvial inventories¶





Figure 3. Continental SAD envelope curves for drought (top) and pluvial (bottom) events with different durations (3, 6, and 9 months, represented by different markers) based on SMPct. For a particular duration (e.g., 3-month), the curve is generated by selecting the maximum bound of severities from all drought events (e.g., left panel of Figure 3). Each curve can be made up of different episodes (represented by different colors), as some events are more severe for large areas while others could be more severe for localized events (with smaller areas).
Note: Events identified by SPI can be found in the Supplementary Material of He et al. (2020).
